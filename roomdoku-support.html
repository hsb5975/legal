About the App

Roomdoku is a logic puzzle game where you place suspects across a grid of rooms by deducing from a list of clues — a hybrid of Sudoku-style constraint solving and a whodunnit mystery. Each puzzle has a unique solution, and once you've placed everyone correctly the missing cell reveals the victim.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Tap a suspect from the bottom pool to select them, then tap a cell to place them. Long-press a cell for the candidate menu (✓ "could be here" / ✗ "ruled out") to track your deductions. Use the clue list at the top to narrow down where each suspect can stand. Hit Submit when every suspect is placed and you'll find out who the culprit is.

What is the daily streak?

Solving at least one puzzle per day extends your streak. Streak Freezes (earned from rewarded ads or purchasable in the shop) cover one missed day automatically. The streak resets to 0 only after two or more consecutive missed days with no freezes available.

What are hints and how do they work?

There are three hint types:

  • Reveal Cell — places one unplaced suspect at their correct cell.
  • Reveal Culprit — flags the murderer in the suspect pool, but you still place everyone yourself.
  • Undo Wrong — removes every misplaced suspect from the board so you can start that part of the deduction over.

You start with a small hint inventory; more can be earned via daily missions, friend referrals, and rewarded ads, or purchased in the shop.

I bought a 5-pack of Streak Freezes but only see 3.

Fixed in version 1.2.5+ — paid purchases now grant the full count. The 3-token visible cap only applies to free top-ups via rewarded ads. Please update to the latest version; if the missing tokens don't appear after the update, contact support.

How do I link my progress to a Google or Apple account?

Open the app, then go to Profile → Edit Profile → Linked Accounts. Tap "Link" next to Google or Apple ID and complete the sign-in. Your hints, streak, and solved puzzles will then be backed up to the cloud and restored automatically when you reinstall or sign in on another device. Sign in with Apple is currently iOS-only.

What happens to my progress if I sign out?

Signing out wipes the local data on the current device and starts a fresh anonymous session. Your progress remains safe in the cloud under your linked account — sign back in to restore it. Device-level preferences (theme, language, sound, push toggle) are preserved.

I signed into a different account and lost my data.

This shouldn't happen — when switching accounts the app pulls the new account's cloud snapshot. If you signed in with the wrong Google/Apple account by mistake, sign out and sign back in with your original account to restore that account's progress. If both accounts share the same device, contact support with the date / approximate solve count and we'll investigate.

How do I cancel the "Remove Ads" purchase or get a refund?

Remove Ads is a one-time non-consumable purchase. Refunds are handled by Google Play / Apple App Store directly, per their refund policies (typically within 48 hours of purchase). Visit Google Play refund or Apple's Report a Problem page to request one. Hint and Streak Freeze packs are consumable and not refundable once granted.

How do I add friends?

Profile → Friends → tap the "+" icon. You can search by friend code (visible on each player's profile) or accept incoming requests. Once you and a friend are connected, you'll see each other's daily streaks, leaderboards, and can send "nudges" to encourage continued play.

The app is crashing or behaving unexpectedly.

Please try the following:

  • Update Roomdoku to the latest version from the store.
  • Restart your device.
  • If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall (your progress restores from cloud after sign-in).

Still having trouble? Email us with your device model, OS version, and a brief description of what happened.

How do I request a new feature or report a bug?

We love hearing from players. Email us at the address below with as much detail as you can — screenshots and step-by-step reproduction notes are especially helpful for bugs.

How do I delete my account and all data?

Profile → Edit Profile → scroll to the bottom → "Delete Account". This permanently removes your cloud progress, friend list, team membership, and Firebase account. The action is irreversible — once deleted there is no way to recover the data.
